Tips to Maintain Low Stress

  • Keep a Balanced Diet: Eat nutritious foods that support your mental and physical health.
  • Stay Active: Regular exercise, like walking or yoga, helps in maintaining your low stress levels.
  • Practice Mindfulness: Take time to meditate and engage in breathing exercises.
  • Stay Connected: Socialize and build strong connections with your friends and family.
  • Get Enough Sleep: Ensure 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to keep your mind and body refreshed.
